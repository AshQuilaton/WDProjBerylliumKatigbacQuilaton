THE END:

The End is a mysterious, floating dimension inhabited by Endermen and home to the mighty Ender Dragon. It is full of floating islands and unique structures.

AREAS:

End Islands: Small floating islands made of End Stone with scattered obsidian pillars and chorus plants.

– OBSIDIAN • CHORUS PLANTS • ENDERMEN –

End Cities: Rare structures found on outer islands, filled with loot and shulkers.

– SHULKERS • LOOT • ELYTRA –
