THE OVERWORLD:

The Overworld is the main dimension where the player starts their world. This is the most familiar dimension in the game where most of their gameplay happens. This dimension features several events, which include the day-and-night cycle, dynamic weather, and a massive variety of biomes and creatures that come with it.
